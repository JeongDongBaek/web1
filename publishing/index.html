DONGBAEK

A man who tries to possess a true heart

Put the food I want on the skewer in order, and set the black barbecue sauce to 1:1 ratio.It is more delicious if you apply your teeth evenly. It is better for children who do not eat vegetables.
Thinking about the party in my mouth soon, I was all fired up. She got a phone call when the fish went sideways and tried to get it back out. I counted exactly three seconds before answering the phone. I tried to mix my nasal sounds to hide my trembling voice and I mistook it for a neat and polite voice. I became a poor interviewer and asked her many questions, " What do you usually do on the weekends? " " If you prefer to take a walk? " " What food do you like to eat? " When she baked the skewers at the temperature she imagined, closed and opened her eyes, an empty skewer was sticking into my tongue.

I'm very interested in you. The parts you think are the drawbacks are all good to me. Your interest has grown and grown, creating a sweet, huge world, and it has swallowed me. As I stepped back, every word you spilled became a strong step, and I tried to fold my foot in curiosity. I followed my footsteps and I met you again, and I knocked on the door.
